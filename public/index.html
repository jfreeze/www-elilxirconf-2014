ElixirConf is a two day conference on September 1-2, with an optional training day on August 31.

The conference includes five training courses, which provide six hours of hands-on instruction.

The speaker portion of the conference includes three tracks of talks, 31 speakers and 3 keynote speakers.

ElixirConf is a conference for Elixir, OTP, Web and Embedded developers from around the world. Focused on advancing the Elixir programming language and the communities surrounding it, ElixirConf brings together developers, from novice to experienced, to discuss, collaborate, and socialize.

The resort offers unique Disney Differences with character appearances, theme park events, Disney Institute seminars, and much more.
