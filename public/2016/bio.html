Frank Hunleth Troodon Software fhunleth fhunleth

Frank designs embedded Linux-based software for products in many industries including medical, consumer, telecommunications and defense. Frustrated with repeatedly implementing parts of OTP, he started the Nerves project to make it easier to use Erlang and Elixir in embedded development projects. He is an active contributor to open-source. Some of his other projects are Elixir/ALE, nerves_uart, fwup, and erlinit. When not programming, he runs marathons and organizes CoderDojoDC, a free programming club for young people in the Washington, DC area.
